Language companion

The Audio Bear

A comforting companion designed to bring your language into everyday routines. Whether it is used in a reading corner, during quiet time, or at bedtime, it gives young children a tactile, screen-free way to hear familiar voices.

7 touch points. Multiple recordings can be loaded onto each one. Fully offline.
Touch pointsMapped to hands, feet, ears, and tummy
RecordingsCommunity-owned recordings approved by your community
UseHomes, classrooms, and language nests
Power3 AAA batteries (replaceable)
Built without screens, Wi-Fi, or apps. Recordings are loaded offline so the bear arrives ready to use, with no extra setup required.
Hands-on language learning

Offline Audio Flashcard Reader

Simple on purpose. Insert a card and the reader plays the matching word or phrase in your community’s voice. Children can repeat what they hear, and educators can build daily language routines around it, all without screens.

UseInsert a card to hear the recording
CardsDurable laminated decks, with custom options available
RecordingsMatched to each deck and loaded offline
Best forClassrooms, language nests, and home use

Process

Your language. Your voices. Your process.

I do not source language. I help turn your approved recordings into resources that children can use at home, in classrooms, and in language nests.

01

Start with a call

We talk through what you need and how you want it used.

02

Record

03

04
