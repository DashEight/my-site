Indigenous-led learning toys
that carry language, voice, and culture.

Indigenize Toys turns play into a language space. Communities control the words, voices, and recordings. The goal is simple: make it easier for children to hear and practice their language in everyday life.

What it is

Interactive toys (including a talking plush prototype) built to support language learning through touch + audio.

How it works

You provide audio (or we help create it). The toy is programmed with your language and used in homes and learning spaces.

Status

Working prototypes + active partner conversations. Selected as an MIT Solve solution.
