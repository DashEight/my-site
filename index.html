Technology Meets Language Revitalization

We repurpose English-speaking toys into Indigenous language learning tools by embedding microcontrollers and custom audio. Through playful hacking, we empower children and communities to reconnect with their ancestral languages using hands-on, accessible, and culturally-rooted technology.

Community Toy Hacking

We host workshops teaching youth and families how to rewire, code, and repurpose everyday toys into tools for Indigenous language learning.

Indigenized Toys for Sale

Each toy is embedded with carefully curated language audio, recorded in collaboration with fluent speakers and knowledge keepers. Proceeds support further language and tech workshops.

3D Language App

Explore your surroundings through an Indigenous lens. Our app layers 3D environments with land-based names, audio, and visual cues rooted in local languages and teachings.
