Process

Your language. Your voices. Your approvals.

Indigenize is designed to respect community control. We don’t “source” language. We build tools that make your approved recordings usable in real life—reliably, offline, and at scale.

01

Fit + Scope

We confirm your goal, setting, and quantity (pilot, classroom, or distribution).

02

Record + Approve

Your speakers record with your process. You decide what’s approved for children and classrooms.

03

Load + Assemble

We load your approved audio offline and prepare the devices so they arrive ready to use.

04

Deploy

Tools arrive with simple guidance for daily routines—no apps, logins, or setup time.
