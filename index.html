Community-controlled talking flashcards Built to carry language, voice, and culture into everyday play.

Indigenize is a simple system: your word list, your recordings, your approvals. We load your audio into physical learning tools so children can hear and practice language at home and in learning spaces.

What it is

A talking flashcard reader + card sets that play audio when a card is inserted. The same hardware can be localized for different communities—language and recordings are community-controlled.

How it works

1) Choose words/phrases. 2) Record in your voices (we can help). 3) We load and deliver kits for home, classrooms, and language nests. You own the recordings and content—never reused, resold, or shared.

Status

Flashcard readers and decks are the first format shipping sooner. A touch-activated plush companion is in development as a second format using the same approach. Selected as an MIT Solve solution.

Good fit for

Language nests · Nations · educators · community programs · pilot partners looking for a practical tool that is easy to localize and deploy.
